The Free Plugins List

EFFECTS BUNDLES

Kilohearts' Essentials

This is a great FX bundle that has pretty general effects.

Melda Production's MFreeFX

This is another great FX bundle that has general effects along with some specialised effects.
Also contains a great EQ.

GVST's Suite

This is a decent FX bundle, but most are already replaced by the ones above.
I personally recommend getting GClip, GGate, GGrain, GMulti, GTune and GSnap only.

Venn Audio's Free Suite

No-nonsense FX bundle. My personal favourites are FreeClip 2 and FreeConvolve.

REAPER's ReaPlugs

This FX bundle has pretty archaic design, but the compressor, Xcompressor and EQ are stellar.
The delay also has the potential for really cool effects, due to it having practically unlimited delay variations.

Chris Johnson's Airwindows

Pretty great FX bundle containing some unique effects. They have no GUI though, so it defaults to your DAW's wrapper for plugins.

Analog Obsession's Free Plugins

Absolutely amazing analog-modeled plugins!

Andrew Reeman's Spectral Suite

Pretty good spectral processing suite, not sure what else to say.

unplugred's Plugins

Genuinely amazing plugins with a playful UI. The UIs are kinda goofy though.

Spectral Plugins' Plugins

Since Spectral Plugins shut down, their plugins (Pancz, Spacer, OCS-45) are free.

AnarchyRhythm's AnarchyFX

A spectral suite full of some pretty unique plugins.

Ewan Bristow's Plugdata Patches

NOTE: Requires Plugdata.
Really, really good plugdata patches. I don't know what else to say.

STANDALONE EFFECTS
COMPRESSION, SATURATION AND DISTORTION

Xfer Records' OTT

It's OTT. What more can I say?

Sixth Sample's Cramit

This is OTT but with a good mix knob, and pre- and post-FX distortion.

unplugred's Prisma

Very, VERY good multiband distortion supporting 16 one-knob distortion types.

Noise Engineering's Ruina

Deceptively powerful distortion with lots of modes, a filter and modulation to destroy your sound.

Tritik's Krush

Great distortion and bitcrushing plugin.

Wolf Plugins' Wolf Shaper

Simple, very concise waveshaper. Essentially Fruity Waveshaper.

Tokyo Dawn Records' TDR Molotok

Awesome dynamic compressor with vintage emulation for alive compression.

Robbert's Spectral Compressor

Have you ever wanted a 16k band OTT? No? Well, here you go. Seriously great spectral compressor.

Venn Audio's Free Clip

Pretty great clipping plugin that can go from very harsh clips to softer, more saturated sound. Also contains waveform display.

Kilohearts' Compressor

Great run-of-the-mill compressor with a bit more up its sleeve.
It has three different modes, includes gain to bring back the lost decibels from compression and also has the ability for sidechaining.

Kilohearts' Distortion

Stellar run-of-the-mill distortion plugin with six modes, bias, stereo spread and can bring back the dynamics of the original sound.

Kilohearts' Phase Distortion

A distortion unlike any other. Basically FM, but as an effect.

Kilohearts' Dynamics

An upwards and downwards compressor combination.

Kilohearts' Bitcrush

Great standard bitcrush plugin.

Kilohearts' Ring Mod

Great for destroying and trashing your sound in pleasant (and horrible) ways.

Outobugi's Refire

Quick and easy drum processing chain.

STANDALONE EFFECTS
REVERB AND DELAY

ValhallaDSP's Supermassive

Stellar reverb and delay with lots of modes and the ability to create
anything from lush landscapes to otherworldy delays.

ValhallaDSP's FreqEcho

Stellar delay and frequency shifter combination that can descend into worbly chaos.

Sixth Sample's Deelay

Really nice delay with several modes to transform your lingering sound.

unplugred's CRMBL

"Delay for insane people" as it is called, and it holds that title beautifully.

Valdemar Erlingsson's CloudSeed

An amazing algorithmic reverb unlike any other.

Glitchmachines' Hysteresis

Stellar glitchy delay plugin.

Impulse Record's Convology XT

Great convolver, but contains not much impulse responses. The Internet is a thing though, so you can find IRs pretty much everywhere.
Since IRs are just WAV files, you can load pretty much anything into it and create weird FX.

Kilohearts' Reverser

Reverse delay. Great for textures.

Platone Studio's Flex Reverb

Pretty standard reverb with one nifty feature -- a built-in parametric EQ for the reverb tail!

STANDALONE EFFECTS
FILTERS

TAL-Software's TAL-Filter 2

Great LFO-modulated filter, which can also be triggered via MIDI!

Kilohearts' Filter

Standard filter. Great for sweeps, or just quick filtering of sounds.

Kilohearts' Non-linear Filter

An amazing filter with modes for analogue emulation, or nasty madness.

STANDALONE EFFECTS
EQ

Tokyo Dawn Records' TDR Nova

Stellar four-band dynamic EQ.

Tokyo Dawn Records' TDR VoS SlickEQ

Great three-and-a-half-band EQ with EQ models and EQ saturation.

ReJJ's ReEQ

NOTE: Requires JP Cimalando's YSFX to use in other DAWs that aren't REAPER
Basically a clone of one of the greatest EQs of all time, Fabfilter ProQ.

Melda Production's MEqualizer

Great standard 6-band equalizer with included tube saturation for more analogue EQ.

iZotope's Ozone 11 EQ

iZotope and the people at Native Instruments were generous enough to give us a great EQ completely free of charge!

STANDALONE EFFECTS
MASTERING

Alex Hilton's Stereo Control

Great stereo widener with a feature called "Safe Bass" which stops stereo widening at a customisable frequency.

Kilohearts' Limiter

Basic, but good limiter with input and output gain.

Donnerstag's LoudMax

Stellar maximizer and true peak limiter with look-ahead.

D16 Group's Frontier

Stellar maximizer and limiter for getting just a little bit more loudness out of your tracks.

Izotope's Ozone Imager 2

Stellar imager from the guys that created the Ozone suite.
You can use a temporary email service like TempEmail for this.

Venn Audio's Free Clip

This also functions as a stellar mastering clipper with oversampling options!

STANDALONE EFFECTS
ANALYSER PLUGINS

Voxengo's SPAN

Stellar spectrum analyser with lots of options to view your sound.

Janus Thorborg's Signalizer

Stellar all-in-one analyser with an included vectorscope, oscilloscope, spectrogram and spectrum analyser that are all very accurate.

FX23's Psyscope

Very good and simple beat-synced oscilloscope.
If you are on Mac though, you can instead get:

Bom Shanka Machines' occularScope

Yet another good beat-synced oscilloscope, but with sidechain input!

STANDALONE EFFECTS
OTHER

Daniel Gergely's Emergence

Stellar granulator with the ability to descend into utter chaos.

ValhallaDSP's SpaceMod

Stellar flanger with the ability to descend into flanging madness.

Glitchmachines' Fracture

Stellar versatile glitch plugin.

Izotope's Vinyl

Great for those scratches and dust from yesteryear.

Sonic Anomaly's Transpire

A very good transient designer for completely free. Also hard clips at -0.1DBFS.

enummusic's AllPassPhase

Literally Kilohearts' Disperser but free.

Robbert's Diopser

Kilohearts' Disperser but free. Now with a better UI!

Kilohearts' Ensemble

TL;DR: unison but for every single sound that comes through it.

Kilohearts' Transient Shaper

Another great transient designer with a very useful feature -- sidechain!

Kilohearts' Trance Gate

Great gate sequencer for getting a bit more rhythm out of static instruments.

Auburn Sounds' Inner Pitch

Very, VERY good realtime pitch shifter with almost no artifacts.

Polyverse Music's Wider

Great stereo widener.

JD Factory's UltimUtility

Essentially Ableton's Utility as a VST.

Cymatics' Deja Vu

Literally a clone of Cableguys' Halftime but free.

unfamiliartunes' spectralsand (Free Version)

Absolutely stellar spectral plugin that essentially delays little bits of the spectrum by set amounts.

Xfer Records' Dimension Expander

Absolutely amazing stereo widener.

sandr0's sand_stretch

Basically dBlue Stretch, but with more features.

Wildergarden Audio's MAIM

Real-time MP3 compression. Live back in the 2000s!

Lese Audio's Codec

Real-time VoIP compression. Make your music sound like it's going through a shitty TeamSpeak call!

Buffer Override by Destroy FX

Absolutely goated plugin for stutters of all kinds.

tugrulakyuz' TugSpect

Essentially an incredibly powerful drawable filter.

Kaixo's SpectralRotator

VST implementation of Ravarcheon's spectral rotation. Basically allows you to create some REALLY weird shit.

justdanpo's RMSC

Genuinely amazing plugin for sidechain. It uses ring modulation to sidechain, and since it doesn't convert the input audio's dynamics into a curve, it's essentially seamless and doesn't have any kind of delay.

INSTRUMENT BUNDLES

Christian Henson's Pianobook

Wonderful site containing hundreds of sample libraries.

NOTE: You need a plugin like DecentSamples' DecentSampler or Plogue's sforzando to load libraries here.
Native Instruments' Kontakt and Apple's EXS24 are not free.

Versillian Studios' Freeware

Absolutely wonderful orchestral libraries.

NOTE: You need Plogue's sforzando to load some of the free libraries here.

DSK Music's Free Plugins

Some plugins are a hit or miss, but they are pretty nice.
Also contains a soundfont player.

VIRTUAL INSTRUMENTS
SYNTHS

Matt Tytel's Vital

Absolutely amazing synthesiser that shouldn't even be free.

Pascal Gauthier's Dexed

Great synthesiser modeled on the Yamaha DX7.

DISTHRO's Cardinal

Absolutely amazing modular synthesiser. Basically a free VCV rack.

Surge Team's Surge XT

Surge is an amazing synthesiser with a pretty great FX bank also containing some effects from Airwindows.

TAL-Software's TAL-Noisemaker

Great analog synthesizer, and is also pretty beginner-friendly.

u-he's Zebralette

Very powerful one-oscillator spectral synthesizer.

YMCK's Magical8BitPlug 2

Great for when you want to emulate the NES in your modern DAW.

Spitfire Audio's LABS

Orchestral Tools' SINEFactory

VIRTUAL INSTRUMENTS
SAMPLERS

CWITEC's TX16WX Sampler

Pretty much THE BEST sampler you can get for free.
Check out the features here to see what you're getting into.

slowslicing's phasereplicant

Very, very good granular synth with drag and drop and phase adjustment of individual grains.

Steven Slate Audio's SSD5

Pretty great drum machine that contains acoustic samples more suited towards rock, metal, etc.

Decomposer's Sitala

Stellar drum machine with the ability to use separate instruments from a loop, built-in compression, shaping, and more.

OTHER MENTIONS

Bertom Audio

Bertom make great plugins. My personal favourites are Denoiser, Air Shelf and EQ Curve Analyser.

Surge Team's Stochas

Stochas is an advanced MIDI sequencer. Perfect for coming up with new ideas for drums or instruments.

Native Instruments

Native Instruments will sometimes have special events where they put certain plugins for free
like Raum, Ozone 9, or Neutron 3.
Be on the lookout for those, they are genuinely good plugins. Raum is a great reverb.

Robbert VDH's YABridge

For the Linux producers out there (and god bless y'all), this is a WINE VST bridge for running Windows VSTs on your distro.

Freakshow Industries

I'm only including Freakshow here because they have given you the option to steal their plugins. Not even joking.
Seriously though, their stealing page really makes you rethink piracy. Genuinely amazing marketing.
